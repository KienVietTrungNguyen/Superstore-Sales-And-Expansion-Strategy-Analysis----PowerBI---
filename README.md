# Superstore-Sales-And-Expansion-Strategy-Analysis----PowerBI---

## I. Introduction

### 1. Inrtoduction to Dataset
  Dataset: Global Superstore Sales
  
  Dataset stores sales information of some products of a comapany worldwide 
  
  The dataset consists of three tables:
  
  - Orders: contains transaction details.
    
  - People: includes information about sales representatives in each region.
    
  - Returns: records the transactions that were returned.
### 2. Business Question
The Senior Manager wants to understand the company’s business performance in order to develop market expansion strategies and make decisions on selecting strategic products.

The Senior Manager’s needs revolve around three main questions:

- What is the current overall business performance?

- How is the business performance across different markets?

- How are the different product categories performing?

## IV. Insight 

1. Overall Business Performance

Strong growth: Revenue +51.5%, Profit +52.3%, margin stable at ~11.6%.

Operations improving: Transactions +51.7%, return rate slightly decreased (4.68%).
→ Business is scaling efficiently with a solid base for expansion.

2. Market Performance

Core markets: APAC, EU, US contribute ~70% of total profit with stable margins.

High-growth markets: Africa and EMEA show the fastest order growth.

Risk area: EMEA has lowest margin (5.45%), requiring cost optimization.

Expansion opportunity: Canada has highest margin (26.2%) and rising AOV → strong potential for future growth.

3. Product Performance

Technology is the main profit driver with the highest margin (~14–15%).

Furniture has low margins (~4–6%) and may reduce overall profitability.

Office Supplies provides stable margin (~12%) and high sales volume.

4. Customer Insights

Top customers generate a large share of profit.

Some key customers have higher return rates (5–8%), requiring monitoring.

## V. Recommend
1. Strengthen Core Markets

Focus on APAC, EU, and US (≈70% of total profit).

Drive growth through Technology products and cross-selling strategies.

Maintain stable margins (12–13%) while scaling revenue.

2. Pilot Expansion in Canada

Launch a 12-month pilot targeting premium Technology products.

Increase order frequency via loyalty programs, cross-sell, and upsell.

Leverage Canada’s high margin (26%+) and growing customer base.
→ Goal: Turn Canada into a high-margin growth market.

3. Improve Profitability

Optimize Furniture category (reduce costs, improve quality, or phase out low-margin items).

Reduce product returns, especially among key customers.
→ Target +1–2% improvement in overall profit margin.

4. Strengthen Key Customer Relationships

Develop VIP programs for top customers.
